# Apple Q2 FY2026 Earnings Call — Key Highlights

## Revenue & EPS
- Revenue $111.2B, +17% YoY, March record (above guidance high end)
- EPS $2.10, +22% YoY, March record
- Gross margin 49.3% (above guidance high end), +110bps sequentially
- Net income $29.6B, operating cash flow $28.7B

## Segment Revenue
- iPhone $57B, +22% YoY (March record, despite supply constraints)
- Services $31B, +16% YoY (all-time record)
- Mac $8.4B, +6% YoY
- iPad $6.9B, +8% YoY
- Wearables/Home/Accessories $7.9B, +5% YoY

## Key Business Highlights
- Installed base >2.5B active devices, all-time high across all categories
- iPhone 17 family "most popular lineup in history" (launch through March)
- Services: all-time records in most categories, paid+transacting accounts at all-time highs
- Mac: March record for new-to-Mac customers, all-time high installed base
- Greater China: strong double-digit growth

## Capital Return
- Board authorized additional $100B share repurchase
- Dividend raised 4% to $0.27/share
- Returned $15B to shareholders in quarter ($3.8B dividends + $11B buybacks)
- Net cash $62B; company no longer targeting "net cash neutral" as formal goal

## June Quarter Guidance
- Revenue growth guidance: +14% to +17% YoY
- Gross margin guidance: 47.5%–48.5%
- OpEx guidance: $18.8B–$19.1B
- Services growth: similar rate to March (ex-FX)
- Assumes current tariff rates unchanged and macro doesn't worsen
